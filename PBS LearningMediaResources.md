## PBS LearningMedia Resources for 3rd Grade NGSS

The following curated resources from PBS LearningMedia align with Washington State’s 3rd grade NGSS requirements. Resources are organized by performance expectation.

### **Physical Science - Forces and Motion**

#### **For 3-PS2-1, 3-PS2-2 (Forces and Motion)**

**Forces and Motion Collection**  
- https://www.pbslearningmedia.org/subjects/science/physical-science/forces-and-motion/  
- Comprehensive collection of lessons on forces, motion, and interactions for all grades.

**Balanced and Unbalanced Forces**  
- https://www.pbslearningmedia.org/subjects/science/physical-science/forces-and-motion/balanced-and-unbalanced-forces/  
- Resources specifically focused on balanced and unbalanced forces concepts.

**Describing Motion**  
- https://www.pbslearningmedia.org/subjects/science/physical-science/forces-and-motion/describing-motion/  
- Activities and videos about measuring and describing motion patterns.

**Force and Motion | Science Trek**  
- https://www.pbslearningmedia.org/resource/idptv11.sci.phys.maf.d4kfom/science-trek/  
- Explores force and motion concepts through engaging examples including amusement park rides.

#### **For 3-PS2-3, 3-PS2-4 (Magnetic and Electric Forces)**

**Magnetic Force Collection**  
- https://www.pbslearningmedia.org/subjects/science/physical-science/forces-and-motion/magnetic-force/  
- Resources on magnetic forces and interactions for various grade levels.

**Electric Force Collection**  
- https://www.pbslearningmedia.org/subjects/science/physical-science/forces-and-motion/electric-force/  
- Activities exploring electric force interactions.

**Magnets! | PBS LearningMedia**  
- https://www.pbslearningmedia.org/resource/reach-with-stem-magnets/magnets/

**Teaching Beyond the Textbook with Magnets**  
- https://thinktv.pbslearningmedia.org/resource/tdpd12.pd.sci.magbeyond/teaching-beyond-the-textbook-with-magnets/

### **Tips for Using PBS LearningMedia Resources**

1. **Filter by Grade Level**: Most collections allow filtering - look for K-2 and 3-5 grade bands
1. **Adapt Middle School Resources**: Many middle school resources can be simplified for advanced 3rd graders
1. **Combine Resources**: Use videos from one resource with lesson plans from another for comprehensive coverage
1. **Professional Development**: Watch the PD videos first to understand NGSS implementation
1. **Check Alignments**: Each resource lists specific NGSS standards it addresses
1. **Free Access**: All PBS LearningMedia resources are free with educator account registration

### **Additional Resource Discovery**

**Browse by Standard**: Visit https://www.pbslearningmedia.org/subjects/science/ and use the standard alignment filters to find resources specifically aligned to individual 3rd grade NGSS performance expectations.
